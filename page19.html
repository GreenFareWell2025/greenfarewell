Description

Palm trees are characterized by unbranched stems and large, evergreen leaves called fronds. Their flowers are small and white, while their fruits vary by species, including coconuts and dates.

Habitat and Distribution

Palm trees thrive in tropical and subtropical climates and are found in diverse habitats such as rainforests, deserts, and coastal areas.

Uses

  • Culinary: Produces fruits like coconuts and dates, and palm oil for cooking.
  • Construction: Palm wood is used for building materials and furniture.
  • Cultural: Palms symbolize peace, fertility, and victory in various cultures.

Fun Facts

Did you know? Palm trees are not true trees but are more closely related to grasses and bamboo!
